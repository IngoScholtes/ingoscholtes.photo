      Hiking through Valle Verzasca, Ticino

      March 2013
      • Distance
      • 35.1 km
      • Total Ascend:
      • 1036 m
      • Max. Altitude:
      • 938 m
      • Walking time:
      • 8h 38m
      • Days:
      • 2

      As a preparation for our longer tour along the Skye Trail, in March 2013 a colleague and I decided to test our equipment on a two-day hike in Ticino. We decided to cross the valley of the Verzasca river, a beautiful and rather isolated mountain area just north of Lago Maggiore.

      Those interested in following the tour themselves can find detailed GPS track data here.

      Day 1: From Sonogno to Lavertezzo
      • Start:
      • Sonogno
      • End:
      • Lavertezzo
      • Distance:
      • 18.5 km
      • Ascend:
      • 461 m
      • Max. Altitude:
      • 938 m
      • Walking time:

      On the first day, we took a bus from the train station in Tenero to Sonogno in the mountains of Valle Verzasca. From there we followed the trail close to the river until Lavertezzo, which is situated near some picturesque cascades of the Verzasca river. We spent the night in a cottage house high above the river.

      Day 2: From Lavertezzo to Tenero
      • Start:
      • Lavertezzo
      • End:
      • Tenero
      • Distance:
      • 16.6 km
      • Ascend:
      • 575 m
      • Max. Altitude:
      • 889 m
      • Walking time:
      • 4h 07m

      The second stage takes us along the river from Lavertezzo towards Lago Maggiore. The trail follows the river until Corippo. From there it winds up into the foggy forests. A beautiful trail takes us along ruins of cottage houses and picturesque villages, before we finally arrive at Tenero.
