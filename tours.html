      Day Hike Collection

      Philosophers, scientists, and writers have instinctively noted the deep connection between walking and thinking for more than two millennia. Recent studies corroborate this intuition, showing that regular walks through nature improve our ability to think, help us to develop creative solutions and support both physical and mental health. To benefit from those positive effects, I go on day hikes whenever I can and wherever I am. On this page, I share a collection of my favorite routes that I have discovered over the past years.
