      Bringing the great outdoors inside.

      pho|to|pos

      As much as I love spending time outdoors to explore natural landscapes, I also enjoy virtually exploring new places through beautiful large scale maps. With this on-going series I intend to convey both fascinations at once via a new type of virtual exploration. Being a portmanteau of the words "photo" and "topos", the term photopos refers to a photographic mapping of a real or imaginary place, area, or country.
