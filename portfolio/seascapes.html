      Seascapes

      Around the world, beaches, cliffs, coves, and bays are regarded as places of contemplation and — as we say in German — "Fernweh", literally the "longing for the faraway". They mark the end of land - and thus of human influence and power - and designate the beginning of the wild, untamed sea. This and their sheer beauty explains the fascination that inevitably befalls us whenever we stand at the water's edge.
