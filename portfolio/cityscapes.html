      Cityscapes

      Even though I prefer nature devoid of human influence, human-created landscapes in villages, towns or cities can be fascinating as well. As you can easily tell from the selection below, I mostly enjoy cities at dusk or at night, when either the twilight of the blue hour or artificial lights illuminate streets and buildings. Whenever I stay in a city during my travels I have a tripod with me and chances are that you can find me wandering around at night.
