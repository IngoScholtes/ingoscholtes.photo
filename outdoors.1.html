Outdoors

There is no better way to explore, experience and photograph natural landscapes than on trekking or hiking tours. Over the past years, I have done single- and multi-day tours on foot, bike and canoe. Here you can find detailed information for some selected journeys. I also provide some photographic impressions that hopefully inspire you to enjoy the 'great outdoors' by yourself.
