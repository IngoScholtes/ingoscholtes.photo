      Videography

      Apart from photography, I increasingly enjoy shooting video. I not only appreciate the new opportunities that the time dimension adds to photographic composition and expression. I also enjoy the novel aerial perspectives now becoming accessible thanks to compact multicopters. As I take my first steps in videography, on this page I present some initial results.

      Alpenpanoramaweg

      Some impressions from the summit of Stöcklichrüz near Lake Zurich. This video was shot in early spring during a hike along the Alpenpanoramaweg from Lachen, Zurich to Einsiedeln, Schwyz. Shot with a DJI Mavic 2 Pro drone.

      Braunwald Panorama Trail

      The following video was shot with a DJI Mavic 2 Pro drone while hiking along the Panorama Trail in Braunwald, Glarus in the Swiss Alps. We did this trail on a marvelous sunny day in late autumn.

      Alpstein and Säntis

      The Alpstein mountain massif is one of my favorite areas in Switzerland. On a sunny day in September, we did the challenging mountain hike from Ebenalp to the summit of Säntis, a mountain with a height of 2501 meters. The following video was short from the first part of the trail, close to Ebenalp. Shot with a Mavic 2 Pro drone.

      Bettmeralp and Aletsch Glacier

      In the winter of 2018 we spent a few days in the area around Aletsch, Switzerland's largest glacier. On a hike along a winter hiking trail, I shot this video using a Nikon D750 with Atomos Ninja Flame and a DJI Mavic Pro drone.

      Stauberen and Alpstein

      This short video shows some impressions from our two-day hike in the Alpstein area. It was shot with a DJI Mavic Pro drone as well as a Nikon D750 with Atomos Ninja Flame recorder and Syrp Genie mini timelapse controller.

      Seealpsee

      One of Switzerland's most beautiful mountain lakes lies at the foot of Säntis mountain in the Alpstein massif. In summer, it is the perfect place for wild swimming. The following video was shot during a mountain hike from Wasserauen, Appenzell via Seealpsee to Aescher mountain inn. Shot using a DJI Mavic Pro drone.

      Scenic Switzerland

      This is a collection of scenes from different areas in Switzerland, including Rigi mountain, Lake Lucerne, Flumserberg, Bachtel, Hallwil Castle, Zugersee, Walensee, and Amden. The video was shot using a Nikon D750 with Atomos Ninja Flame recorder as well as a DJI Mavic Pro drone.
